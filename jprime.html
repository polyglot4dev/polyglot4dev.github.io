Polyglot4dev conferences

for jPrime visitors

Nayden Gochev April 15, 2022

This is a unique conference, first of its kind conference, where visitors already familiar with a particular language or technology can actually LEARN in a fast way other languages and technologies!

The goal is to improve ourselves, not to be focused only on one technology stack, to be able to use the best tools for the job, and to be able to think differently and see the bigger picture.

Technology stacks come and go, the same happens for frameworks, languages, and tools, but we are the ones that stay and wanna choose. Always wanna learn, but you never wanna lose !

So if you always wanna go, but you never wanna stay. Always want to learn new things and want to get out of your comfort zone. Join us on a UNIQUE journey where we will explore new worlds, new languages, frameworks, package managers and etc.

Become a polyglot developer TODAY!

The second edition is for all us Web developers out there that want to learn or at least see the other programming languages and technology stacks.

2023 - the year of the web polyglot4web conference by polyglot4dev ;)

It will happen in person, 26th October 2023 at Inter Expo Center, Sofia! If you are a person who wants to learn how to write React or want to learn WTF is the 'Dart' side of the force does .NET or Blazor have a future OR is Angular better then Vue or simply wants to understand what this black magic called webpack and who in the world will prefer TypeScript over JavaScript. then this is the conference for YOU !
